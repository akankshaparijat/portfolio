Go HASTEN: A GenAI based solution for eco-friendly travel
Akanksha Parijat
March 05, 2023
Overview
The Sustainable Travel Planner application is a user-friendly platform that allows individuals to create personalized travel plans while prioritizing low carbon footprint. By leveraging GEN-AI technology, the application offers tailored recommendations for destinations, transportation options, accommodations, and activities, ensuring an enjoyable travel experience with minimal environmental impact.

Background
Traditional travel planning often overlooks the ecological consequences of tourism. As the demand for sustainable travel grows, there is a need for a comprehensive solution that integrates carbon-conscious decision-making into the travel planning process. The Sustainable Travel Planner application aims to address this gap by combining user preferences and GEN-AI to optimize travel plans with reduced carbon emissions.

My Contribution
As the lead designer of the Sustainable Travel Planner application, my role involved conceptualizing, designing, and overseeing the development of the user interface and experience. I collaborated closely with a cross-functional team of developers, researchers, and domain experts to ensure the application's effectiveness and usability.

Research
During the research phase, several key findings emerged regarding space tourism to Mars. While the application primarily focuses on Earth-bound travel, understanding the emerging field of space tourism is crucial to future-proof the platform. Key findings included the potential environmental impact of space tourism, the anticipated technological advancements in space travel, and the evolving market landscape.

Problem Statement
The lack of readily available information and tools to help travelers create sustainable travel plans tailored to their preferences is a significant challenge. Existing travel planning platforms often prioritize convenience over environmental considerations, leading to increased carbon emissions. The goal is to develop an application that seamlessly integrates personal preferences with carbon footprint management, promoting sustainable travel choices.

Target Audience
The Sustainable Travel Planner application targets environmentally conscious travelers who value personalized experiences without compromising their commitment to reducing carbon emissions. This includes both leisure travelers and business professionals seeking to align their travel plans with their sustainability goals.

Ideation
During the ideation phase, various concepts were explored to integrate the application's core functionalities effectively. Brainstorming sessions and user feedback were used to refine ideas and prioritize features that align with user needs and preferences while considering carbon reduction.
Design Concept
The final design concept involves a user-friendly interface that allows travelers to input their desired destinations, travel dates, accommodation preferences, and transportation options. Using GEN-AI technology, the application generates personalized travel itineraries that prioritize low carbon footprint, providing recommendations for eco-friendly hotels, public transportation, and activities.


Internal Evaluation
The Sustainable Travel Planner application includes several key features, including:
1. Carbon Cost Management: Users can easily manage and adjust the carbon cost of their travel plans, with real-time carbon footprint calculations for each aspect of the itinerary.
2. Destination Recommendations:GEN-AI analyzes user preferences and suggests sustainable travel destinations that match their interests, while considering carbon emissions.
3. Transportation Options:The application offers various low-carbon transportation options, including public transport, shared rides, and eco-friendly vehicle rentals, based on the user's location and itinerary.
4. Eco-Friendly Accommodations:Users are provided with recommendations for eco-certified accommodations that prioritize sustainable practices and carbon neutrality.
5. Activity Suggestions: The application suggests eco-friendly activities and experiences at each destination, considering their environmental impact and alignment with user preferences.


Bias Analysis
A thorough bias analysis was conducted throughout the design process to ensure fairness and inclusivity. The AI algorithms were regularly audited and tested for any biases, and steps were taken to mitigate and address potential discriminatory outcomes, such as biased destination recommendations or transportation options.

Design Reflection
The Sustainable Travel Planner application has successfully achieved its goal of empowering users to create customized travel plans while minimizing their carbon footprint. The integration of GEN-AI technology has enabled a seamless user experience, providing personalized recommendations and real-time carbon cost management. Ongoing user feedback and continuous improvements will be prioritized to further enhance the application's functionality and address evolving sustainability challenges in the travel industry.
